Ingredients

  • 2 cups of self-raising flour
  • An egg
  • A cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • vanilla essence(optional)

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour and sugar
  2. Add the milk to to flour and sugar mixture
  3. Melt the butter and it to the mixture in the bowl
  4. Mix it well and add the vanilla essence
  5. Place your pan on medium heat and rub in some oil
  6. Using a spoon, scoop the mixture from the bowl and drop it on the pan
  7. Cook both sides until it turns golden brown
  8. Place the cooked dropscones in a bowl lined with kitchen paper towels
  9. Serve when hot with tea
